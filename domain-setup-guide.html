üîß Troubleshooting
Q: My domain isn't working after setup

DNS changes take time to propagate (up to 48 hours). Try clearing your browser cache or testing in incognito mode.

Q: I see "DNS_PROBE_FINISHED_NXDOMAIN" error

This means DNS records haven't propagated yet. Wait a few hours and try again.

Q: I see "ERR_SSL_VERSION_OR_CIPHER_MISMATCH"

SSL certificate is still being provisioned. This usually takes 15-30 minutes after DNS is active.

Q: Can I use a subdomain like portfolio.mydomain.com?

Yes! Just use "portfolio" instead of "@" in the CNAME record name field.

Q: Do I need to keep paying my old registrar?

Yes, if you're using Option 2. Cloudflare just manages the DNS, you still own the domain at your registrar.
